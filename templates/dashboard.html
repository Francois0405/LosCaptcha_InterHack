Recommendation logic

AI-supported decision context

Group dense urban clients

One stop can serve several nearby customers if the parking point and delivery window are compatible.

Prioritize early accessibility

Products for first stops stay closest to lateral truck access to reduce handling effort.

Reserve returnable space

Empty crates and kegs can come back during the route, so the load plan keeps flexible space available.
